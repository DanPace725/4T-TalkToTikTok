Talk to TikTok

This application allows you to download and transcribe TikTok videos. Simply enter the URL of the video and the app will do the rest.

Proceed

How it works

1. Enter the URL of the TikTok video you want to transcribe.

2. The application will download the video and convert it to an audio file.

3. The audio file is then transcribed using advanced AI technology.

4. You will receive the transcription of the video in text format.
